Hear No Evil 🙉

A rhythm puzzle-platformer with turn-based movement.

gameplay clip — coming soon

It's a rhythm-based game with turn-based movement. Every move lands on the beat — whether that's solving a puzzle, getting through a platforming section, or fighting something.

The setup: a troupe of monkeys summoned three idols through song and dance, and now have to stop them to get peace back.
